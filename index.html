About This Site

Welcome! This is my personal collection of study notes on Cognitive Psychology, with a focus on Embodied Cognition, language, perception, and the relationships between mind, brain, and body.

These notes are based on university lectures, academic readings, and my own reflections as I explore how thinking and meaning are shaped by our sensory and motor systems.

Topics Covered

  • Language and the brain
  • Disembodied vs. embodied meaning
  • Symbol grounding problem
  • Simulation theory
  • Brain areas for perception, action, and language
  • Fodor vs. Barsalou: Competing mind models
  • Research strategies in embodied cognition
